Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Agrilink Farmer, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and location details you provide during registration.
  • Media: Photos and videos you upload to showcase your produce.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including features accessed and time spent on the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable farmers to list and manage their produce effectively.
  • To connect farmers with buyers and facilitate transactions.
  • To improve app functionality and enhance user experience.
  • To send important updates, notifications, or promotional content.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • With trusted service providers who assist in app operations (e.g., payment processing or delivery services).
  • When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement industry-standard measures to secure your data, including encryption and secure servers. However, no system is entirely foolproof, and we encourage users to protect their account credentials.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal information through your account settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be communicated via in-app notifications or updates on this page.
